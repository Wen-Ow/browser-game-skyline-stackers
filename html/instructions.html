How to Play Skyline Stackers

Welcome to Skyline Stackers, where the classic block-stacking gameplay you love meets the thrill of designing a glowing city skyline! Step into the role of an urban architect and strategically stack and clear blocks to light up towering skyscrapers. Your goal is simple: stack pieces to clear lines, score big, and keep up with the pace before the blocks pile too high. Are you ready to build the city of your dreams? Let's get started!

  1. Start the Game: Click the "Play Game" button to begin.
  2. Controls: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the tetris blocks.
    • Left Arrow: Move block to the left
    • Right Arrow: Move block to the right
    • Down Arrow: Move block down faster
    • Up Arrow: Rotate block
  3. Clear Lines: Align blocks to fill horizontal lines completely to clear them.
  4. Track Progress: Watch your score and level increase as you play. As you clear more lines, the game will get faster.
  5. Game Over: If the blocks reach the top of the screen, the game is over.
  6. Reset Game: Click the "Reset Game" button to restart and play again.
  7. Pause Game: Click the "Pause Game" button to pause the game while playing.
